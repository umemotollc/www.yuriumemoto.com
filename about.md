Yuri Umemoto (b. 2002) is a composer born in Tokyo. His music is built through notation, recording, and editing, often incorporating recorded voices and songs. He creates works ranging from stage performances to recordings.  

2026 Residency Fellow, Akademie Schloss Solitude, Stuttgart, Germany.  

Recent performances include 'Concerts' by Isengard Guilde and Office PSR, Brussels, Belgium and Paris, France (2026); Les Siestes, Toulouse, France (2026); CCA Berlin – Center for Contemporary Arts, Berlin, Germany (2026); portrait concerts at Les Urbaines, Lausanne, Switzerland (2025, 2026); Ensemble Resonanz at resonanzraum St. Pauli, Hamburg, Germany (2026); Felicity J Lord at Cafe OTO (presented by Kiosk Radio), London, UK (2026); Minor Attractions at Avalon Cafe, London, UK (2026); Solistenensemble Kaleidoskop at Radialsystem and Sammlung Hoffmann, Berlin, Germany (2025); Brother Tree Sound at Kings Place, London, UK (2025); PODIUM Esslingen, Germany (2024–2026); and Thüringer Bachwochen, Germany (2024).
