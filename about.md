Yuri Umemoto (b. 2002) is a composer born in Tokyo. His music is built through notation, recording, and editing, often incorporating recorded voices and songs. He creates works ranging from stage performances to recordings.　　

A 2026 Residency Fellow at Akademie Schloss Solitude, Stuttgart, Germany.　　

Recent and upcoming projects include crypt_, a new opera commissioned by the Münchener Biennale and co-produced by the Münchener Biennale, Ultima Oslo Contemporary Music Festival, Oslo Sinfonietta, Black Box teater Oslo, and Klang Festival Copenhagen (2026–2028); concerts by Isengard Guilde and Office PSR in Brussels, Belgium, and Paris, France (2026); Les Siestes, Toulouse, France (2026); CCA Berlin – Center for Contemporary Arts, Berlin, Germany (2026); Ensemble Resonanz at resonanzraum St. Pauli, Hamburg, Germany (2026); Felicity J Lord at Cafe OTO (presented by Kiosk Radio), London, UK (2026); Minor Attractions at Avalon Cafe, London, UK (2026); portrait concerts at Les Urbaines, Lausanne, Switzerland (2025); Solistenensemble Kaleidoskop at Radialsystem and Sammlung Hoffmann, Berlin, Germany (2025); Brother Tree Sound at Kings Place, London, UK (2025); PODIUM Esslingen, Germany (2024–2026); and Thüringer Bachwochen, Germany (2024).
