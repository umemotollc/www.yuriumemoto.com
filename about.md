Yuri Umemoto is a composer born in Tokyo in 2002.  

Upcoming project is the new opera [*crypt_*](https://www.muenchener-biennale.de/en/programm/kalender/crypt), commissioned by the Münchener Biennale. The work is a co-production with the Münchener Biennale, Ultima Oslo Contemporary Music Festival, Oslo Sinfonietta, Black Box teater Oslo, and Klang Festival Kopenhagen.  

2026 Residency Fellow, Academy Schloss Solitude, Stuttgart, Germany  

Recent performances include a portrait concert at Les Urbaines Festival, Lausanne, Switzerland (2025); at Radialsystem and Sammlung Hoffmann, Berlin, Germany, performed by Solistenensemble Kaleidoskop (2025); at Kings Place, London, UK, performed by Brother Tree Sound (2025); PODIUM Esslingen Festival, Germany (2024–2025); and Thüringer Bachwochen, Germany (2024).
